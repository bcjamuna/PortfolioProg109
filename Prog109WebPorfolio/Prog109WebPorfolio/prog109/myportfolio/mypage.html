Happy Diwali!

I love to travel, as a child I lived in India for a couple of years. The culture, people, and atmosphere are rich with kindness, color, and flavor. Each year, depending on lunar calendar calculations, from around mid-October to mid-November is the month of Kartik--During this month of Damodara, multiple belief systems celebrate a huge festival called Diwali. This celebration is bigger than our New Year's parties; they have fireworks, lots of food, fun, partying with friends and family in homes and on the streets. Not only is it a time to have fun but it's also a time for worship. I made this gif to share with you all. It's been a great and challenging quarter, good luck everyone!
